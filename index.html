About Home Not Alone

Do you worry about that special someone in your life? Are you faced with distance or have a demanding lifestyle that prevents you from giving your loved one the time and attention they need? Do you feel as if you have too much to do within a short space of time? Would a helping hand make a difference?

A Concierge Service, particularly for seniors, can prove to be advantageous not only to the individual but also to other family members. With help from a Concierge, visiting days can be more about quality time together rather than dealing with responsibilities at every visit, resulting in less stress for all those involved. Home Not Alone will handle the daily chores and errands of everyday life, so that you can get to those moments that are really important.

We can help with daily home tasks, namely domestic housework, preparation and cooking of healthy meals, running errands, shopping or providing a mode of transport to various destinations, such as doctor visits, social engagements or simply just to get out of the house for a reside of hours.

We can also provide a clerical/administrative service whereby we can approach, contact and schedule home repairs and home maintenance with skilled and qualified contractors, should this be necessary. In addition, we can also assist with holiday planning for day trips or family holidays. The list is endless.

Home Not Alone are not limited to just providing a one to one Concierge Service for seniors, but we can also assist less mobile individuals, busy professionals and couples with two incomes but no free time, by doing what they are too busy or unable to do themselves. We can capable and resourceful problem solvers, who are happiest when helping our Clients with whatever challenge comes their way.

Services are customisable and affordable and can vary from Client to Client. Package deals are also available on request. Every situation is different and we welcome the chance to learn about your family dynamics and how we can help you.
